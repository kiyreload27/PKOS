# Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

This directory acts as the decision log for PKOS. With the architecture officially frozen at v1.1, any future deviations or significant new patterns must be logged here along with their rationale.

---

## Example Format

### ADR 001: Example Decision
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Accepted

**Context:**
Why is this decision necessary? What limitation in v1.1 are we hitting?

**Decision:**
What is the specific architectural change?

**Consequences:**
What are the trade-offs, and how does it impact existing systems?
